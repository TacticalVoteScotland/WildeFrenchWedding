About Our Wedding

Travel and Accommodation

Getting to Sheffield

  • The nearest airports with Transatlantic flights are London Heathrow, London Gatwick and Manchester.
  • You can get to Sheffield directly from most major UK cities - it's two hours from London, five hours from Glasgow, and about an hour from Manchester and Birmingham.

Once you arrive at the station, taxis and Uber will be available, or you can take the 52 or 52a bus from the bus station.

Where to Stay

  • Most major hotel chains are located in or near the city centre. The wedding will take place in Crookes, a suburb to the north-west of the city with plenty of Airbnb and Booking.com listings. Nearby suburbs include Walkley, Hillsborough, Broomhill and Ranmoor.

    If you are not bringing a car, try to avoid staying east of the city centre as transport links are more limited.

What can I do when I'm there?

Sheffield is Britain's sixth biggest city, so there's plenty to do nearby! Within the city you can visit the Peace Gardens, Winter Gardens and Millennium Gallery in the City Centre, take a walk out to Forge Dam, or check out lots of eating and drinking options around Kelham Island and Hunters Bar.

If you want to head out of the city, it's right next to the beautiful Peak District National Park, as well as nearby to Chatsworth House and the village of Bakewell (home of tarts).
